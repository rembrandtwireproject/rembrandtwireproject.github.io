Your watermark is Basel Crosier, A.a.b.

Watermark A.a.b. can be distinguished by several features: the top of the basel crosier is on a crowned shield, the central band of the crown contains three groups of triple circles and the initials EHM can be found below the shield. This watermark is a twinmark with Basel Crosier, A.a.a.
