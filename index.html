Welcome to the WIRE project.

This student-designed program is intended to simplify the process of confidently identifying watermarks in Rembrandt’s etchings by offering a guide to the often subtle differences between them.

In its finished form, it will allow researchers and others interested in Rembrandt’s prints to upload watermark images from Rembrandt prints for identification, accessing information about a given impression’s date and membership in an edition.

To start, select a watermark image from the choices offered, and follow the series of questions and images to find a match.

Enjoy!
