Your watermark is Eagle, Single-Headed, B'.a.a. or B'.a.b.

Watermarks B'.a.a. and B'.a.b. are difficult to distinguish beyond these two sub-variants, but both share the characteristics marked in blue: a fleur-de-lis at the center of the eagle, ruffled feathers at the neck, and segmented wings of varying lengths.
