Your watermark is Eagle with Basel Crosier, Single-Headed, B.a.a.

Watermark B.a.a. can be distinguished by three features: its eagle contains a Basel crosier, its foot on the beak side stays in, but does not cross, a chain line, and below it are the letters ID.
