Your watermark is Eagle with Basel Crosier, Single-Headed, B.a.b.

Watermark B.a.b. can be distinguished by these features: a Basel crosier at the center of the eagle, ends of feathers from only one wing crossing the chain line (marked with red), and three claws extending from the foot which are rounded, not pointed at the ends.
